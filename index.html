Dispatch Notes

1) Send signed rate confirmation agreement to the email that dispatcher tendering the load.
2) Carrier/driver is to call broker when arriving to load; if there are any damages, overages or shortages.
3) Drivers are responsible for verifying legal weight of shipment. Both gross weight and axle weight.
4) Any deviation or additional charge must be approved and a modified rate confirmation agreement will be sent.
5) Detention is paid at a rate of $25/hour after 2 free hours. ($200 maximum detention paid).
6) Layover is paid at $150 per day.
7) In order to avoid late charges, any delays must be reported at the time of delay.
8) The rate confirmation can not be used as a BOL.
9) Failure to accept tracking will cause in charges $200.
